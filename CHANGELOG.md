# Changelog

All notable changes to this book and its companion materials will be documented here.
This project follows a simple **Edition.Revision.Patch** versioning scheme:

- **Edition** — substantive rewrite or new Parts added.
- **Revision** — new annotations, case-law updates, new translations, restructured sections.
- **Patch** — typos, citation fixes, broken links, formatting.

---

## [1.0.0] — 2026-04-16

### Added
- First open-access edition published on Internet Archive.
- Five-part structure: Philosophical Foundations · Failures of Existing Law · Technical Realities of AI · The Mali Doctrines · The Seven Laws of Artificial Intelligence.
- CC-BY 4.0 licence.
- Formats: PDF, EPUB, full-text TXT, DAISY (accessibility), HOCR.

### Source
- https://archive.org/details/seven-ai-laws-the-future-of-mankind-by-prashant-mali
